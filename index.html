// Who We Are

About
GBG

GBG (Go Broke Gang) is one of Los Santos' most feared crews on the FiveM server. We started with nothing and built an empire on loyalty, grit, and raw street hustle.

Our colors are black and white — because there are no grey areas when it comes to respect. You're either with us or you're not.

We run the streets, run the rackets, and ride together no matter what. If you want in, you better be ready to earn your stripes.

// Code of the Streets

Gang
Rules

  • 01

    Loyalty First

    Gang members come before everything. You bleed black and white — no exceptions, no excuses.

  • 02

    No Snitching

    What happens inside GBG stays inside GBG. Anyone caught talking to the cops is out — permanently.

  • 03

    Respect the Ranks

    Follow the chain of command. Higher ranks make the calls. You earn your say by earning your rank.

  • 04

    Represent the Colors

    Wear black and white. Don't dishonor the gang by dressing sloppy or acting outside the code.

  • 05

    Stay Active

    Ghosts don't eat. Show up, participate, and contribute. Inactive members get removed — no hard feelings.

  • 06

    Handle Business

    We handle our beef internally. No public drama, no petty beef. Keep it professional, keep it clean.
